Manifesto
Our brand lives by a conscious mindset. Not just when it comes to creating quality clothing and goods, but as we strive for quality in everything we do.

Hailing from Birmingham’s creative heartbeat, the Jewellery Quarter, the brand has been built in a place synonymous with quality. It’s historic, hardworking & hardwearing attitude continues to fuel our passion for creating clothing that will stand the test of time.

Working closely with our factories across the globe, we continually push ourselves to create the best clothing, as sustainably as we can. At P&Co we live by 3 core values - creativity quality & pride. These 3 values help focus, drive & unite us all, not only as a brand but as people.
